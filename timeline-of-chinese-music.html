Timeline of Chinese Music


Description


The Interactive Timeline of Chinese Music details Chinese music history in dynastic periods.


The program covers the following topics:

  • Han to Qing Dynasty
  • Republic Period
  • Initial Communist Era
  • Cultural Revolution
  • Period of Openness to Present
